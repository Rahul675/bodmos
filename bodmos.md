# Bodmos Child Safety Standards Policy

**Effective Date:** May 2026

Bodmos is committed to ensuring a safe, secure, and lawful environment for all users. We enforce a strict zero-tolerance policy against any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE).

We explicitly prohibit the creation, upload, sharing, promotion, or facilitation of any content or behavior that endangers minors.

---

## 1. Zero-Tolerance Policy

Bodmos strictly prohibits:

- Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM), including any depiction of minors in sexual contexts
- Any form of child sexual exploitation or abuse
- Grooming, solicitation, or predatory behavior toward minors
- Sexualization of minors in any form (explicit or implicit)
- Human trafficking or exploitation involving minors
- Any attempt to engage a minor in sexual activity or inappropriate communication
- Content that depicts, promotes, or normalizes harm to minors

**Enforcement Actions:**
- Immediate removal of violating content
- Permanent suspension or termination of user accounts
- Reporting to appropriate law enforcement and regulatory authorities

---

## 2. User Responsibilities

All users must:

- Comply with applicable child protection laws and regulations
- Refrain from uploading, sharing, or engaging with prohibited content
- Report any suspected violations immediately

Failure to comply may result in account termination and legal consequences.

---

## 3. Reporting Mechanism

Bodmos provides in-app mechanisms for reporting:

- Inappropriate content
- Suspicious user behavior
- Child safety concerns

Reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team. We prioritize urgent cases involving potential harm to minors.

---

## 4. Moderation and Enforcement

We implement proactive and reactive safety measures, including:

- Automated content detection systems
- Human moderation and review
- User reporting and escalation workflows
- Account restriction, suspension, and banning systems

We continuously improve our systems to detect and prevent abuse.

---

## 5. Legal Compliance

Bodmos complies with all applicable child safety and protection laws, including obligations related to:

- Detection and reporting of CSAM
- Cooperation with law enforcement agencies
- Preservation of evidence where required by law

We cooperate fully with regional, national, and international authorities in investigations involving child safety.
