Bodmos Child Safety Standards Policy

At Bodmos, we are committed to maintaining a safe and secure platform for all users and strictly prohibit any form of child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, trafficking, or any harmful behavior involving minors.

Zero-Tolerance Policy

Bodmos maintains a zero-tolerance policy against:

Child sexual abuse material (CSAM)
Child exploitation or abuse
Grooming or predatory behavior
Sexualization of minors
Human trafficking involving minors
Any content or behavior that violates child safety laws

Any user found engaging in such activities will be permanently removed from the platform and reported to the relevant authorities.

Reporting and Moderation

Users can report inappropriate content, suspicious behavior, or child safety concerns directly within the app.

Our moderation and safety systems include:

User reporting tools
Content review and moderation
Account suspension and banning mechanisms
Cooperation with legal and law enforcement authorities where required
Compliance With Laws

Bodmos complies with all applicable child safety laws and regulations, including reporting obligations related to child sexual abuse material and exploitation.

We cooperate with regional, national, and international law enforcement agencies when legally required.
